Project Name: Work in Progress
Year: 2019

My final project for my third semester in Bachelor of Creative Technologies (BCT). For this project I took on a team leader role as well as acting as lead technician. I used the program ReactiVision working in tandem with Unity to create an immersive digital display which was altered depending on the presence and location of objects on the table in front of the user.
