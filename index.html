About Me

I am a Bachelor of Creative Technologies Graduate in Tāmaki Makaurau/Auckland, New Zealand. I use an iterative approach to create projects that bring together multiple disciplines, working efficiently in a team to create outcomes that would not be achieved using traditional methods. I also minored in game design, and have created several small digital games, which can be viewed on my itch.io page.
